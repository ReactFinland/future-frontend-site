Conference format One of the aspects that makes Future Frontend unique in its category is the format we use for the event. Over the two days of the event, we run eight themed sessions with two speakers each. Each pair gets a 1.5 hour slot and usually this means two talks and a panel on the topic. Beyond this, we have made sure you have enough time to recharge during breaks as for some people the hallway track is the best part of the conference as you get to meet other developers. On top of this, we take care to organize more unofficial program after conference days with our partners and an afterparty for Friday is guaranteed. Themes for 2024 For 2024, we have chosen the following themes: compilers, artificial intelligence, design, UI in combination with design and edge, green computing, standardization, user interaction, and demonstrations. In other words, there will be plenty of inspiration available especially for a frontend-minded developer.
